Roberta Muri

I am a 4th-year PhD student in Economics at the University of Bologna and an Economist at Prometeia. Starting in March 2026, I will be a Visiting Researcher at the California Institute of Technology in Pasadena.

My research focuses on the effectiveness of climate adaptation policies at the local level, using spatial data and applied econometric methods.
