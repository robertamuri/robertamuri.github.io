I am a PhD Candidate in Economics at the University of Bologna and an Economist at Prometeia. I am currently a Visiting Researcher at the California Institute of Technology in Pasadena, hosted by Hannah Druckenmiller.

My research focuses on the effectiveness of climate adaptation policies at the local level, using spatial data and applied econometric methods.
